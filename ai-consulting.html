Common Questions

What people ask first

Do we need to be tech-savvy to benefit from this?+
No. Most of our clients are communicators and creatives — not technical teams. We build systems that are simple to use on the other side, even if the setup is complex. If your team can post on social media, they can run what we build.
Will AI replace any of our staff?+
Not the way we approach it. AI handles the repetitive, time-consuming parts of content work — transcription, formatting, scheduling, rough cuts. Your team keeps ownership of the strategy, the voice, and the relationships. Most of our clients find their team gets more creative, not smaller.
What tools do you use?+
We're tool-agnostic — we recommend what's right for your workflow and budget, not what we prefer. Common tools we work with include Descript, Opus Clip, Make (Integromat), Zapier, Notion AI, ChatGPT, Claude, CapCut, and various platform-native tools. We don't push expensive enterprise software.
How long does implementation take?+
A workflow audit and roadmap takes 1–2 weeks. A single AI system build typically runs 2–4 weeks from kickoff to handoff. Full implementation of multiple systems across a 3-month engagement is the most common structure for organizations serious about transformation.
What does AI consulting cost?+
Workflow audits start at $1,500. Full system builds range from $3,000–$8,000 depending on complexity. Ongoing retainers for continued optimization and support start at $750/month. We'll give you an exact proposal after the discovery call.
What if we've already tried AI tools and they didn't work?+
This is the most common situation we walk into. Tools don't fail — implementation does. We'll audit what you tried, understand why it didn't stick, and build a system designed around how your team actually works — not how the tool assumes you work.
